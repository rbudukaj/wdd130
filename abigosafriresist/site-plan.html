Wireframes

Create three wireframes for your site. One for each page and list them here

Home

Hero section with an introductory banner, welcoming visitors to the site. A featured content section highlighting key articles, videos, or stories related to the celebration of African resistance and empowerment. Navigation menu with links to the other main topics and important pages. A section for recent community discussions and engagement. A footer with contact information and social media links.

African Icons

A dedicated page with an introduction to the topic. A grid or carousel of images representing various African icons with brief descriptions. Clicking on an icon's image or name leads to a dedicated profile page with more information about their contributions. Navigation back to the main "African Icons" page.

Historical Movements

A dedicated page with an introduction to the topic. A timeline or interactive map highlighting significant movements and uprisings throughout African history. Clicking on a timeline event or a map location leads to detailed articles or pages about each historical movement. Navigation back to the main "Historical Movements" page.

Contemporary Voices

A dedicated page with an introduction to the topic. A dynamic feed featuring content about present-day African voices in social justice and empowerment. Profiles of contemporary activists, leaders, or events. A section for visitor contributions, allowing users to share their own stories or insights. Navigation back to the main "Contemporary Voices" page.
