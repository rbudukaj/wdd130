Introduction

Welcome to Abigo’s Afri Resist, a website dedicated to celebrating the rich culture and history of Africa while also exploring the theme of resistance. Our goal is to provide a platform for people to learn about the struggles and triumphs of African people throughout history and to inspire them to take action against injustice. Whether you’re interested in art, music, literature, or politics, you’ll find something to engage with on our site. Join us in our mission to promote awareness and change!” We hope this helps! Learn about individuals who have made significant contributions to African culture, history, and society.
These icons come from all walks of life and have
excelled in fields such as politics, music, literature, art,
and sports. Some of the most well-known African icons
include Nelson Mandela, Chinua Achebe, Fela Kuti,
Miriam Makeba, and Haile Selassie. These individuals
have inspired generations of Africans and people
around the world with their courage, creativity, and
leadership. They have left a lasting legacy that
continues to shape African identity and culture today.

Voices are the voices of today’s generation. They are
the writers, artists, musicians, and activists who are shaping
the world we live in. At Abigo’s Afri Resist, we believe that it’s important to amplify these voices and provide a platform for
them to be heard. Our contemporary voices section features interviews, articles, and creative works from some of the most exciting and innovative voices in Africa today.
